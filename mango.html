Kimaye Alphonso Mangoes

The beloved mango variety known for it's matchless aroma and flavour, Alphonso is often given the epithet "The King of Mangoes." Also known as Hapus locally, the variety is a perfect serve for the table. Each Kimaye Alphonso mango is GI tagged, ripened carbide-free, traceable to it's farmer and source and comes with a minimum guaranteed weight and size.

FRUIT FUN FACTS

Alphonso mango got it's name from a Portuguese general & military expert "Alphonse de Albuquerque". In 2006 George. W. Bush tried an Alphonso Mango during his first visit to Inida. After having it, he said to the then prime minister Dr. Manmohan Singh that it was “a hell of a fruit’, the best he ever tasted.
