FRUIT FUN FACTS

Strawberry is the only fruit with seeds on the outside and the first fruit to ripen in the spring. Ancient Romans believed that the fruit symbolized Venus, the Goddess of love. There is a museum in Belgium dedicated to strawberries and May 21-27 is celebrated as Strawberry Week annually in Delaware.

HEALTH BENEFITS

Strawberry has compounds offering benefits to ageing brain. The fruit possess anti-inflammatory properties and boosts cardiovascular health. Strawberries are also known to inhibit the growth of cancer cells.
